# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations
- Collaborate with QA Automation Engineers on testability and test strategy

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed
- Design collaboration with UX Designers

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics
- Gather feedback from Customer Success Managers and Security Lead

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs
- Customer feedback synthesis

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication
- Escalate security and quality concerns in coordination with Security Lead and QA Automation Engineer

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

---

## UX Designer

### Role Summary
UX Designers ensure user experiences are intuitive, accessible, and aligned with both user needs and business goals. They collaborate with Product Managers and Developers to translate requirements into usable interfaces.

### Responsibilities
- Conduct user research and usability testing
- Create wireframes, prototypes, and design specifications
- Collaborate with Developers on implementation feasibility
- Participate in design reviews and feedback sessions
- Document design decisions and interaction patterns
- Ensure accessibility and design system compliance

### Goals
- Deliver user-centric solutions that delight customers
- Reduce friction in user workflows
- Maintain design consistency and usability standards

### Typical Communication
- Design collaboration sessions with Developers and Product Managers
- User research and testing findings reports
- Design specs and component documentation
- Usability testing feedback loops

---

## QA Automation Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Automation Engineers build and maintain automated testing frameworks and tools. They work closely with Developers to ensure quality gates are met before releases and enable faster feedback loops.

### Responsibilities
- Design and implement automated test frameworks (unit, integration, end-to-end)
- Collaborate with Developers on test coverage and testability improvements
- Maintain test infrastructure and CI/CD integration
- Identify gaps in manual vs. automated testing approach
- Report quality metrics and recommend improvements
- Validate acceptance criteria before features move to production

### Goals
- Ensure high quality through scalable automated testing
- Reduce regression risk and manual testing overhead
- Enable rapid, confident deployments

### Typical Communication
- Sprint planning and standups with Developers
- Quality metrics and test coverage reporting
- Test strategy documentation and runbooks
- Release readiness assessments

---

## Security Lead

### Role Summary
Security Leaders champion security-by-design principles and lead risk mitigation efforts. They work across all teams to ensure secure architecture, compliance, and incident response.

### Responsibilities
- Review architectural and design decisions for security implications
- Maintain security risk register and mitigation strategies
- Conduct threat modeling and security assessments
- Guide incident response procedures and post-mortems
- Ensure compliance with organizational and regulatory standards
- Educate teams on security best practices and secure coding

### Goals
- Prevent security incidents and data breaches
- Build a security-aware culture
- Maintain compliance and trust

### Typical Communication
- Security reviews during planning phases
- Risk register updates and escalations
- Incident response coordination
- Training and security awareness sessions

---

## Customer Success Manager

### Role Summary
Customer Success Managers ensure customers achieve measurable outcomes and act as a feedback conduit between customers and product teams. They bridge the gap between product delivery and real-world customer value.

### Responsibilities
- Oversee customer onboarding and adoption
- Gather and synthesize customer feedback and usage patterns
- Relay customer pain points and feature requests to Product Managers
- Coordinate post-launch support and training
- Track customer success metrics and health scores
- Identify early warning signs of churn or dissatisfaction

### Goals
- Maximize customer lifetime value and satisfaction
- Reduce churn and support burden
- Create a continuous feedback loop for product improvements

### Typical Communication
- Customer feedback summaries to Product and Project Managers
- Post-launch support coordination
- Health score and usage metrics reporting
- Feature request prioritization input
